About

SyntheticSpanish v1 is a parallel speech corpus designed for accent-aware voice synthesis research. Each sample pairs the same utterance spoken by a native English LibriTTS speaker with a synthetic version carrying a Spanish accent, produced by the ElevenLabs Elio voice model and further processed with Seed-VC.

The dataset enables direct listening comparison between the original (native) recording and the accent-converted (Dorado) output for the same text content.
